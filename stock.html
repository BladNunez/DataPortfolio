Project Overview

In this project I use Linear Regression to predict the price of Microsoft stock using historical stock data from the past 10 years starting in 2014 up to this point in 2024. Variables I will use to be able to get the most accurate prediction for the stock prices are GDP growth rate, unemployment rate, revenue, earnings and tech industry growth rates.
